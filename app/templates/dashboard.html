How to subtitle a film

Two manual flows on the Library page — Subtitle This never auto-translates anything on its own, and there is no whole-library "subtitle everything" button on purpose:

  1. One item: find the row you want, click Subtitle this. The job appears below in the jobs list.
  2. A custom batch: tick checkboxes on the rows you want (works across pages — the selection is remembered), then click Subtitle selected in the sticky toolbar above the table. One job is queued per ticked item.
